Hello! Welcome to the answer page for the fifth problem!

So for this quibble, you can choose any two corner pieces, but let's move the top most and left most pieces. Imagine a line going through the left middle and right middle pieces and place one of them to the right of them. With the remaining piece, imagine a line through the left middle and bottom middle pieces and place the second piece to the bottom right extension of it, just under the right middle piece. Et voila, we have an upside down triangle!
